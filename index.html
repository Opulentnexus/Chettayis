ABOUT US

Perfectly crafted catering and event plans.

Chettayis Events began in 2018 with one goal — to deliver high-quality, value-focused event management and catering. After the setback of the 2020 pandemic, we returned stronger, supported by the trust of our clients. Today, we’ve proudly served over 1,000 customers across Kerala. We handle every detail with care, professionalism, and a personal touch — like an elder brother guiding your special day. Whether it’s a wedding, corporate event, or celebration, we create memorable experiences that let you relax and enjoy every moment.
